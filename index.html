"Two men Keys, vocals, and drums: Simple and effectful"

Take control, sweat it out,you're a man not a civilized monkey - "Sylvester Larsen" (Also known as "Sly") sings on the duo's first official single "Back To Nature" The single is the first taste of their upcoming debut album "Back To Nature" is about the big city man's longing for a more simple life in harmony with nature. The synth-dominated track very much exemplifies the edgy and melodic sound that characterizes Sly and The Man

Sly and The Man is an alternatice synthop/rock duo from Copenhagen, Denmark and Berlin, Germany. Although the single is the first one out on Spotify, Sly and The Man has already gauned a large international audience with more than 200,000 views on their single "The Mission" on Youtube

Behind the name is the composer and lyricist Sylvester Larsen (keys and vocals) and drummer Martin Hulz Larsen (drums, percussion, and vocals) Sylvester Larsen has a long career as a songwriter and eight albums under his belt. His latest release is "Trolddom" (2021). Martin Hülz Larsen has his musical roots in German rock and metal and has worked as a freelance drummer with phenomenons such as Haudegen, Sprinter X, and Undrowed
